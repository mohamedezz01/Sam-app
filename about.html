About Us

SAM is your ultimate destination for all your real estate needs in Egypt. We understand that buying a property can be a daunting task, and that's why we have created a user-friendly platform that makes the process easy and hassle-free.

Finding the right price for a house can be challenging.. Buyers: Risk overpaying or missing out on good deals due to inaccurate pricing. Sellers: Risk underpricing and leaving money on the table or overpricing and scaring away potential buyers. This is where our house price prediction project SAM comes in!

For buyers, knowing the accurate market value of a property enables informed decision-making, preventing overpaying for a property or missing out on a good deal due to inaccurate pricing. On the other hand, sellers benefit from accurate price prediction by ensuring they neither underprice their property, leaving money on the table, nor overprice it, which can deter potential buyers. SAM addresses these challenges to attend to the needs of both buyers and sellers.
